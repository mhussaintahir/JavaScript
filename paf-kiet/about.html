    About:


Our mission is to educate the whole person, graduating students who seek purpose-filled lives. Our academic programs are designed to prepare students to meet challenges of the fast-changing and developing world.

The PAF-KIET Main Campus, spread over 22 acres, is situated at PAF Korangi Creek, Karachi. It is located within 15-20 minutes drive from multiple points (Baloch Colony Overpass and FTC) on Shahrah-e-Faisal and is easily accessible. The educational complex at this campus has custom-built infrastructure that enables professional education in a peaceful environment.

 

Purpose built building structures, open spaces and green lawns, and diversity of students creates a stimulating and lively campus environment.

The PAF-KIET City Campus is situated in Block-6 P.E.C.H.S at Shahrah-e-Faisal, Karachi. The location provides opportunity to working students for acquiring higher professional education during evenings and weekends.
